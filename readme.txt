Zagzig integration extension for WooCommerce.

== Description ==

Zagzig Integration allows WooCommerce users to automatically update their Zagzig Partner Portal with sales information so that no leads needs to be updated manually. 

Zagzig is a social referral platform that promotes your products and services, while combining social interaction and cash incentive. Our platform makes it easy for customers to recommend your offers to their social network and allows your products to reach 1000s of Zagzig users who will share your offers with their friends and family, reaching a larger crowd and resulting in more sales.
